MUSIC IQ QUIZ

Music IQ is a quiz that displays 10 random music questions. The questions can vary from types of instruments to artist details. You will have 15 seconds to answer each question and if the time runs out, the question will be counted wrong.

Music IQ Rules:

  • 1. Use your ninja skills to answer the questions!
  • 2. You will be given 10 random questions that are related to music. They can be anything from questions about musical instruments to the color of a particular artist's hair!
  • 3. You will have 15 seconds to answer each question.
  • 4. When time runs out, the question will be counted incorrect.
  • 5. You will see your total score after you have answered 10 questions.
  • 6. Good luck and have fun!!.
